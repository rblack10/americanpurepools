American Pure Pool Service: Auburn CA

American Pure Pool Service has been servicing pools in Auburn, CA for over 10 years. Auburn families rely on our expertise, experience, and fair rates to service their pool.

Auburn is located in the foothills of the Sierra Nevada mountains. It is the county seat of Placer County, California. During the last census, it had a population of 13,330.

Auburn is considered part of the greater Sacramento area and is home to the Auburn State Recreation Area, a site hosting more sporting endurance events than any other place in the world.
