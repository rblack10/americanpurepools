American Pure Pool Service: Rocklin CA

Although based in the foothills in Meadow Vista, American Pure Pool Service services pools in Rocklin, California. Rocklin families rely on our pool servicing, fair rates, experience, and the expertise that goes with being in the business for over 15 years.

Rocklin is a city of 56,000 people located in Placer County, California located a little over 20 miles from Sacramento up towards the foothills. It is near Granite Bay, Loomis, and Lincoln.

Rocklin has an interesting history associated with the transcontinental railroad. When the railroad was completed in 1869, rail traffic significantly increased through Rocklin, helping the city grow and expanding commerce.
