About American Pure Pool Service & Repair

At American Pure Pool Service, we believe there is a better way to service pools. A more valuable, less invasive way where customers can trust their technicians to be professional, reliable, and honest. We're obsessively passionate about serving our community with all of their pool service needs. We focus on pool service and repair in Meadow Vista, Auburn, Rocklin and all the other communities in between. We're excited to provide exceptional pool service to everyone with our various pool services and commitment to quality.

We have been cleaning, maintaining, and repairing pools for over 20 years. Our services include, but are not limited to, pool cleaning, water testing and balancing, and pool equipment installation and repair.

My wife and I raise our two kids in the Meadow Vista area, where we have been members of the community for several years. My extensive experience has taught me that a pool is an investment, and I am here to protect that investment with outstanding customer service and care. I work hard to ensure that we always have the time and skills necessary to best serve you. I am always on call for existing customers with urgent questions, even on my vacations and holidays. I am a member of a network of local professional specialists who have expertise and experience to assist me with any jobs or questions that might come up during the care of your pool. I do all I can to ensure the job gets done in a timely fashion and, more importantly, the right way the first time.

I look forward to working with you!

Weekly Service

At American Pure Pools, we understand that all pools have their own personality, so we tailor each weekly service to your pools unique water chemistry and equipment.

Respect for privacy and trust in your pool service technician are built into every customized service plan.

Our upfront pricing will NOT lock you into any binding contracts, and will be tailored to your specific pool service needs.

After each visit, we'll leave a door hanger to fill you in on the health of your pool and our visit.

Most weekly service includes the following:

  • Test water and adjust accordingly
  • Empty skimmers
  • Brush and net
  • Equipment inspection
  • Empty pump basket
  • Filter clean and backwash
