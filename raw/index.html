Startup

American Pure will be happy to send one of our experienced technicians to your house and have them assist you with opening your pool. They will also provide tips on how to easily open your pool in the future. Your pool is an investment, keep it safe with American Pure Pool Service & Repair!

Weekly Service

We feel it is very important to not only understand all aspects of water chemistry, and the pools equipment, but to also respect our customers privacy and their trust in their pool service technician. We are up front with our cost, and DO NOT have any binding contracts. Since all pools take on their own personality, we customize our service based on that, as well as the pool owner needs. Most weekly service includes the following:

  • Test water and adjust accordingly
  • Empty skimmer(s)
  • Brush and net as needed
  • Inspect pool equipment
  • Empty pump basket as needed
  • Clean and/or backwash filter
  • Leave informative door hanger per visit
